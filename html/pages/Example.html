Example

Please refer to examples below to see what w4CSeq can do for you.

Enzyme digestion based 4C-seq

In a mouse cell line, HindIII enzyme was used for fragmenting crosslinked chromosomes, and a 4C library was constructed for Pou5f1(Oct4) gene upstream enhancer for Illumina sequencing.
A demo FASTQ file with 10 million reads from single-end sequencing is used. After the submitted job is completed, you will see the result page linked below.

Enzymatic 4C result (demo)



Sonication based 4C-seq

In a mouse cell line, sonication was used for fragmenting crosslinked chromosomes, and a 4C library was constructed for Pou5f1(Oct4) gene upstream enhancer for Illumina sequencing.
Two demo FASTQ files each with 10 million read pairs from paired-end sequencing are used. After the submitted job is completed, you will see the result page linked below.

Sonication 4C result (demo)
